GetSetUp

GetSetUp is an online platform educating older adults with new skills. However, it is not easy for a popular and big digital community to handle all processes. DOS is a management expert and does the same for GetSetUp by equipping their business with some of the finest virtual assistants for everyday tasks.

About the Client

This platform invests in older people with modern-day tools to help them learn and connect at a bigger horizon.GetSetUp is the top-ranking and fastest growing online community which was founded in 2019. It has grown to more than 4 million members in 160+ countries worldwide with over 2000 instructors.According to your interests, you will find live classes, social hours, events, and more. If you want to learn a new skill, no matter how old you are, GetSetUp is your one-stop-shop.

Business Challenges

This mission-driven American technology company was looking for a versatile team of virtual teaching assistants who could instruct and manage the live classes. VAs have already changed the way how companies conduct business nowadays. GetSetUp was also looking for the same, given their speedy growth.Furthermore, they required a team to oversee all the emails, chats, video conferences, and everything else. To tackle the delayed messages, spammed emails, connectivity issues, unread texts, conflicting time zone, they turned to DOS for round-the-call customers support, especially virtual assistants.

Solution

DOS trained a committed team of over 40 agents that provided 24/7 tier 1 and 2 phone support services. Moreover, they also worked as teachers’ assistants and maintained the connectivity between teachers and students. Our phone support covers the GSU partners too.

Our responsibilities included:

  • Answering thoroughly through 24/7 available chat support
  • Generating effective leads for their website
  • Handling web traffic productively
  • Overseeing back-office processes

DOS VAs play a key role in helping startups, and early-stage companies get off the ground. We manage more than 50 classes daily and provide 24/7 live customer support to ensure brand presence.
